Meet the Team

About Us

Who are We?

We are a group of students dedicated to protecting the Pasig River, a vital waterway with great environmental, cultural, and historical significance for our communities. Through this website, we aim to inspire meaningful actions that will restore the river to its full potential.

Our Mission

To educate and inform the public about the environmental, cultural, and historical importance of the Pasig River while inspiring communities to take action in preserving and restoring it.

Our Vision

A revitalized Pasig River that is:
1. Pollution-Free: A river free from waste and harmful pollutants.
2. Flourishing with Life: A thriving ecosystem with rich biodiversity restored to its former glory
3. A Source of Pride: A symbol of sustainability, embraced and cherished by all communities it touches.

What We Do

Historically, the Pasig River was home to a diverse array of aquatic life, including fish, mollusks, and aquatic plants. However, decades of pollution, industrialization, and urban development have caused significant biodiversity loss. Today, we work to deliver knowledge about the remaining ecosystems and advocate for measures to restore native species and habitats.
